RECITALS
EuroPay Processing GmbH is a licensed payment processor operating under PCI-DSS Level 1
certification. This Agreement governs the provision of payment gateway and transaction
processing services for the Customer's European e-commerce operations.

ARTICLE 1 — SERVICES
1.1 Service Provider shall process card-present and card-not-present payment transactions on
    behalf of Customer via the Payment_Gateway integration endpoint.
1.2 Access type: read-write to Payment_Gateway systems.
1.3 Data handled includes cardholder data, billing addresses, and transaction history.
    Classification: HIGH sensitivity (financial/PII).

ARTICLE 2 — DATA PROTECTION
2.1 The Service Provider confirms that it handles personal data of EU data subjects on behalf
    of the Customer. A GDPR Data Processing Agreement has NOT been executed between the parties
    at the time of this Agreement. The parties acknowledge this as an open action item.
2.2 The Service Provider confirms:
    - SOC 2 Type II certification: VALID, issued 1 March 2024, expiry 28 February 2027.
    - ISO/IEC 27001: VALID, certified by BSI Group.
    - GDPR DPA: NOT EXECUTED (see 2.1 above).
2.3 The Service Provider is not currently under investigation by any regulatory authority.

ARTICLE 3 — FINANCIAL INFORMATION
3.1 Service Provider's Dun & Bradstreet financial strength rating: A- (low risk of default).
3.2 Annual contract value: EUR 320,000 (~USD 348,000 at prevailing rates).

ARTICLE 4 — INCIDENT NOTIFICATION
Service Provider shall notify Customer of any data breach or suspected unauthorised access
within 24 hours of the Provider becoming aware, and in any event within 72 hours as required
by GDPR Article 33.

ARTICLE 5 — GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by German law. Disputes shall be resolved before the courts of
Frankfurt am Main, Germany.
