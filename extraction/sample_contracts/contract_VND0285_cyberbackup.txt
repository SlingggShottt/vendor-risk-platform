MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT
Vendor: CyberBackup Solutions Inc.
Client: Acme Enterprises Ltd.
Contract Reference: MSA-2023-0285
Effective Date: June 1, 2023
Expiration Date: June 1, 2026

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
CyberBackup Solutions Inc. ("Vendor") shall provide managed backup and disaster recovery services,
including full access to Client's primary database servers (Database_Primary) and corporate file
servers (FileServer_Corporate) for the purposes of data replication, snapshot management, and
recovery orchestration. Access type: read-write.

2. DATA CLASSIFICATION AND SENSITIVITY
The Vendor will handle data classified as HIGH sensitivity, including personally identifiable
information (PII), payroll records, and customer financial data. The Vendor acknowledges that
all data processed under this Agreement is subject to the Client's Data Classification Policy
(Tier 1 — Confidential).

3. COMPLIANCE AND CERTIFICATIONS
3.1 The Vendor represents that it holds a valid SOC 2 Type II certification, most recently
    renewed on September 15, 2023, valid through September 15, 2026.
3.2 The Vendor does NOT currently hold ISO/IEC 27001 certification.
3.3 This Agreement does not include a GDPR Data Processing Agreement (DPA), as the Vendor
    processes data on behalf of EU-based subsidiaries of the Client. A GDPR DPA is pending
    and will be executed as a separate addendum within 30 days of this Agreement.
3.4 The Vendor is NOT subject to any known regulatory investigations as of the Effective Date.

4. BREACH NOTIFICATION
The Vendor shall notify the Client of any confirmed or suspected security breach within 72 hours
of discovery. The Vendor warrants that it will maintain an incident response plan and conduct
annual penetration testing.

5. FINANCIAL STANDING
Vendor's current credit rating is B (stable), as certified by the Vendor's most recent annual
financial review dated March 2023.

Data sensitivity: HIGH (PII, financial records)
